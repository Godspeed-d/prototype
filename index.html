A complete horse training & boarding facility

Green Pastures Stable and Training is a full-service training and boarding facility, located just 30 miles east of Cincinnati in Amelia, Ohio. Whether you show competitively, trail ride, or just want a relaxing experience with your horse, we have a program to suit your needs.

  • Fully lit indoor riding arena for year-round use
  • Daily turnout and high-quality feed
  • Personalized training programs available
